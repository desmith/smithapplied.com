Tubular Safety Shower

Introducing our Tubular Safety Shower, designed to meet ANSI standards, ensuring the highest level of safety and reliability. The tubular safety shower system is engineered to deliver a consistent and effective water spray for rapid and thorough decontamination in the event of chemical exposure or eye-related emergencies. Additional features that is added include a scald protection valve, freeze protection valve, stainless steel foot pedal actuator, in-line strainer, and a drench hose.

Key Features:

ANSI Z358.1-2014 Compliance:
Our Tubular Shower and Eyewash are designed to comply with the ANSI Z358.1-2014 standards.

Effective Decontamination:
The design of the tubular shower ensures a consistent and uniform water spray, facilitating efficient decontamination in emergency situations.

Scald Protection Valve:
Enhance user safety with the optional addition of a scald protection valve. This valve ensures that water temperatures remain within safe limits, preventing the risk of scalding during emergency use.

Corrosion-Resistant Material:
Crafted from advanced materials known for their exceptional corrosion resistance, this tubular safety shower is designed to withstand harsh environments and provide long-lasting durability.
