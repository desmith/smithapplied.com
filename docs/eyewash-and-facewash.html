Emergency Eyewash Station

Introducing our Emergency Eyewash Station for safeguarding your workforce in the event of accidental exposure to hazardous materials. Designed with precision and built for reliability, our eyewash station ensures quick and efficient eye rinsing to minimize the impact of chemical splashes or debris. Additional features that is added include a scald protection valve, freeze protection valve, stainless steel foot pedal actuator, in-line strainer, and a drench hose.

Key Features:

Rapid Response:
Engineered for simplicity, our eyewash station features an intuitive activation mechanism, allowing users to initiate the eye washing process with ease.

Durable Construction:
Crafted from high-quality, corrosion-resistant materials, our eyewash station is built to withstand the rigors of industrial environments.

ANSI Z358.1-2014 Compliance:
Our Tubular Shower and Eyewash are designed to comply with the ANSI Z358.1-2014 standards.

Scald Protection Valve:
Enhance user safety with the optional addition of a scald protection valve. This valve ensures that water temperatures remain within safe limits, preventing the risk of scalding during emergency use.
