Passively Cooled Cabinets and Shelters

Passively cooled shelters and cabinets are equipped with our patented FastCool™ passive cooling technology to provide electricity-free and maintenance-free cooling. It uses night cold temperature via heat pipe system to cool down the thermal storage inside the shelter or cabinet which is used to cool the shelter interior in day time. The Cooling system for cabinet is designed to keep the equipment cool even during the hottest summer.

This is a very cost effective solution for many application specially for the remote areas where power is not available. The product is suitable to install in both hazardous and non-hazardous areas due to no electrical parts involved. This lower temperature will help increase the life of equipment without need of an electric cooling system.

Key Features:

  • The shelters and cabinets are made of UV resistance, corrosion resistance GRP material. Stainless steel shelters and cabinets are also available upon request.

  • High quality composite material to build GRP shelters and cabinets for higher durability and strength.
  • Heat pipe system is made of copper or aluminum.
  • Sizes are available as per customer needs.
  • Low maintenance

Applications:

  • Electronic Instrument shelter
  • Analyzer Enclosures
  • Telecommunication shelters
  • Emergency evacuation breathing device cabinet
  • Battery room
