Passive Cooled Safety Tank Shower
ZEC1200 and ZEC1500

This passively cooled emergency shower will provide a contnuous flow of 15 GPM. FastCoolTM passive cooler maintains the temperature of the water in the tank within ANSI limits even in hottest summers. This patent pending technology utilizes a combination of heat transfer augmentation techniques to achieve very fast cooling effects.Thus, FastCool™ system achieves a higher cooling capacity than that of a 3 kW electric chiller typically used in tank showers. Commissioning time of the shower after each use is thus extremely short and the shower will be ready for use within the same shift of the next shift.

The product is suitable to install in both hazardous and non-hazardous areas due to no electrical parts involved. Heat pipe system is made of copper tubes and aluminum fins. The water tank and support structure are made of corrosion-resistant materials.
