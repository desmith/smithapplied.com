ZERO ENERGY COOLER

Safety shower cooler for Hazardous and non-hazardous areas

HOW IT WORKS

ZeroEnergyCooler™ cools water by dispelling heat to the cooler ambient air outside during the night. Commissioning time is short and the cooler can reduce the temperature to ANSI limits within just a few hours.

ADVANTAGES

ANSI Compliant: ZeroEnergyCooler™ provides tepid water within the specified temperature range even during the hottest summer.

Energy Savings: Since no power is involved, ZeroEnergyCooler™ saves energy that is otherwise wasted in cooling the safety shower for 5 months of the year.

No Moving Parts, No Maintenance: The design of ZeroEnergyCooler™ contains no moving parts, requiring significantly less maintenance. Vapor compression chiller and pumps add additional items to a maintenance departmentâ€™s checklist.

Low Installation Cost: No electrical components are required parts for the cooler to operate. This reduces the cost of installation significantly.

Long Life: Life expectancy of ZeroEnergyCooler™ is more than 30 years.

Light Weight: ZeroEnergyCooler™ is lightweight, thus eliminating the need of additional structural support when retrofitted on an existing tank shower.

Hazardous Area Compliant: ZeroEnergyCooler™ can be used for both hazardous as well as non-hazardous areas.

APPLICATIONS

TANK SHOWERS

ZeroEnergyCooler™ can be easily integrated into both new and existing tank showers.

TUBULAR SHOWERS

Tubular in-line new and retrofit: To cool an existing tubular shower, a central chilling plant and water recirculation system is needed. Both the initial installation as well as the ongoing maintenance and continuous cooling requirements are very costly.
